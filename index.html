// ABOUT

CRAFT
FIRST.

I'm a solo developer and small-team collaborator obsessed with the intersection of systems design and player psychology. Every mechanic is a question. Every level is an answer.

Started modding Quake at age 13. Never really stopped. I've shipped titles on Steam, itch.io, and mobile — from pixel platformers to procedural survival sims. Currently prototyping a narrative dungeon-crawler in Godot 4.

I care about feel. Game feel. The satisfying crunch of a well-timed dodge. The quiet dread of a sound design cue. The loop that makes you say "just one more run."

// HOW I WORK

THE
LOOP.

01
💡
CONCEPT
Every game starts with one mechanic that feels interesting. I prototype it in 48 hours to test the core loop before anything else.
02
🔧
BUILD
Iterative development with playtest checkpoints every two weeks. Systems before content. Feel before features.
03
🎮
PLAYTEST
I watch players, not metrics. First-contact confusion reveals more than any analytics dashboard ever could.
04
🚀
SHIP
Shipping is a skill. Version 1 is never done, but it ships. Post-launch patches are part of the design process.
